About the Professional Browser Teleprompter

What is a Teleprompter?

A teleprompter (also known as an autocue) is a display device that prompts the person speaking with an electronic visual text of a speech or script. Using a teleprompter allows presenters to maintain eye contact with their audience (or the camera lens) while reading their lines fluently, eliminating the need to look down at notes or memorize long scripts. Our browser-based tool brings this professional broadcasting technology to your computer for free.

Why Use an Online Teleprompter?

Whether you are a YouTuber, a corporate presenter, or a student, our teleprompter helps you deliver a more polished performance:

  • Improved Confidence: Speak naturally without the fear of forgetting your next point. The smooth auto-scroll ensures you keep a consistent pace.
  • Eye Contact: By placing your screen near the camera, you can read your script while appearing to look directly at your viewers.
  • Mirror Mode for Hardware: If you are using a professional teleprompter rig with a beam-splitter mirror, our "Mirror Horizontal" and "Mirror Vertical" modes allow you to flip the text so it appears correctly on the glass.
  • Focus & Clarity: Use the "Focus Line" to highlight the exact sentence you should be reading, preventing your eyes from wandering across the screen.

Advanced Features for Broadcasters

Our tool includes specialized features designed to mimic high-end studio software:

  • Customizable Speed & Font: Adjust the scrolling speed in real-time using your keyboard or the HUD. Choose high-contrast themes like "Green on Black" or "Amber" to reduce eye strain in dark recording environments.
  • Countdown Timer: Give yourself a few seconds to breathe and look at the lens before the script starts moving.
  • Pause on Hover: A unique feature that lets you instantly stop the scroll simply by moving your mouse over the text—perfect for ad-libs or natural pauses.

100% Private & Client-Side

Privacy is critical when working with sensitive speeches or unreleased video scripts. Most online teleprompters save your text to their servers.

The Teleprompter tool is 100% Client-Side. Your script is processed entirely within your browser's local memory. No data is ever uploaded to a server, and your text is cleared as soon as you close the tab. This ensures that your intellectual property remains completely secure and under your control at all times.
