About the Tip & Bill Splitter

Why Use a Tip Calculator?

Calculating gratuity and splitting a check after a long meal should be the easiest part of your dining experience. Whether you're navigating different service standards or simply want to ensure everyone pays their fair share, the Tip Calculator provides an instant, error-free breakdown. By automating the math, you avoid the awkwardness of manual calculations and ensure that service staff are rewarded accurately for their work.

Mastering the Split

Our tool is designed to handle common dining scenarios with precision:

  • Customizable Gratuity: Use the interactive slider to adjust the tip percentage based on the quality of service. While 15-20% is standard in many regions, you have total control from 0% to 50%.
  • Group Splitting: Dining with friends? Enter the number of people in your party to see the exact amount each individual owes, including their portion of the tip.
  • Real-Time Updates: As you adjust the bill amount or tip percentage, the breakdown updates instantly, giving you a clear view of the total cost before you reach for your wallet.

The Math of Gratuity

The calculation follows a simple but essential formula. The tip amount is calculated as:
$$\text{Tip Amount} = \text{Bill Subtotal} \times \left( \frac{\text{Tip Percentage}}{100} \right)$$
This amount is then added to the original bill to find the total. If splitting, the final amount is divided by the number of participants. Our tool handles the rounding to the nearest cent so you don't have to.

100% Client-Side Privacy

Your spending habits and dining locations are personal. Many mobile apps and websites track your location and purchase history when you use their financial tools.

The Tip Calculator is a 100% Client-Side application. All calculations are performed within your browser's local memory. No data regarding your bill amounts, tip preferences, or group sizes is ever sent to a server or stored in a database. This ensures your financial privacy is protected while providing a fast, reliable utility that works even if your mobile data connection is spotty at the restaurant.
